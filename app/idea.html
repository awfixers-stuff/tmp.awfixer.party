A New American Politics

Fix theSystem.

Not the left. Not the right. The AWFixer Party exists because the existing political establishment has had thirty years to fix America's foundational problems and has instead made most of them worse.

We are not a protest vote. We are not a single-issue movement. We are a comprehensive political platform built on one premise: that American governance has failed not because of bad people but because of bad incentive structures — and that fixing those structures, honestly and without partisan cover, is the only path forward.

We believe in accountability without cruelty, competition without monopoly, transparency without naivety, and consequences without waste. We believe the American people are capable of handling the truth about what is broken and what it will take to repair it. We intend to tell them.

Our Platform — Ten Pillars
01 Competition & Markets Real deregulation means breaking monopolies, not protecting them.
02 Foreign Policy Diplomacy first. Military force reserved for direct, unmistakable threats to Americans.
03 Labor & Immigration Fix the incentive to exploit foreign labor. The behavior follows.
04 Energy & Nuclear Nuclear is the only honest large-scale clean energy answer we actually have.
05 Fiscal Transparency Every federal dollar on a public ledger. Permanently. No exceptions.
06 Criminal Justice Real consequences. Real rehabilitation. A 77% recidivism rate is not tough — it's failure.
07 Anti-Corruption Every branch. Every level. Every uniform. No carve-outs.
08 Technology & AI Open source in public life. Curiosity is not a crime — it should be rewarded.
09 Tax & Business Pro-business means building a tax base that actually works. Not loopholes.
10 Civil Standard of Living Replace the minimum wage with a real, regional measure of dignified existence.
