Our foreign policy is diplomatically approach issues and protecting Americans abroad at all costs. We do not feel inclined to continue to interfere with the Middle East unless a proportionately direct threat to America is glaringly and obviously present. We want to strengthen relationships with leaders like the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Japan and work to reintegrate Russia and China into society instead of continuing to alienate them. We also are considering a formal project and program for the North Koreans as the upcoming leadership change presents a rare foothold to invite them back to the global society
