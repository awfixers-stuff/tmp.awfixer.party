## The Role of Decentralization in Democracy

Decentralization is not merely an administrative choice — it is a prerequisite for meaningful citizen participation in self-governance.

---

## Why Decentralization Matters

When power is concentrated:

- Citizens are subjects, not participants
- Decisions are made far from those affected
- Local knowledge is ignored
- Accountability diminishes

---

## What AWFixer Proposes

Structural decentralization of decision-making authority to the lowest level capable of effective action.
