## Introduction to Our Approach

The AWFixer Political Party is built on a simple but powerful premise: **the quality of outcomes depends on the quality of incentives**.

Every policy, law, and regulation creates incentives. When those incentives are aligned with the desired outcome, good results follow naturally. When they're misaligned, even well-intentioned policies fail.

This is our core insight, and it applies across every domain of governance.

---

## The Incentive Framework

We analyze every policy question through three lenses:

1. **Who benefits?** — Follow the money and power to see who's actually served
2. **What behavior does it create?** — Incentives shape behavior; understand what behavior this produces
3. **Does the outcome match the intent?** — The gap between stated goals and actual results reveals misaligned incentives

---

## Why This Matters

Traditional politics argues about *what* should happen. We argue about *what makes things happen*.

- Minimum wage laws don't raise wages — market dynamics do
- Antitrust laws don't create competition — profitable opportunities do  
- Environmental regulations don't reduce pollution — the cost of pollution relative to compliance does

The law is a tool. The incentive is what actually moves the world.
