## The Absolute Rule of Oligarchy

The history of governance is, in many ways, the history of oligarchy — and the persistent struggle against it.

---

## The Nature of Oligarchic Power

Oligarchy emerges when a small group consolidates disproportionate power. This can happen through:

- Economic control
- Military strength
- Political position
- Institutional capture

The form changes. The pattern persists.

---

## What AWFixer Proposes

Democracy requires constant vigilance against the concentration of power. The solution is not a single mechanism but structural incentives that distributed power broadly.
