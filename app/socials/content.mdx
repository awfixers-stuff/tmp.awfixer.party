## Communication Policy

The AWFixer Party is committed to transparent, consistent, and meaningful communication with the public. This policy outlines how we communicate across our platforms and what you can expect from us.

### Our Communication Principles

- **Transparency**: We communicate openly about our positions, decisions, and reasoning
- **Consistency**: Regular updates across all platforms to keep the community informed
- **Accessibility**: Content is written to be understood by all Americans, not just political insiders
- **Responsiveness**: We engage with the community and address questions when possible

### Response Expectations

While we aim to engage with as many community members as possible, due to volume we cannot guarantee responses to all direct messages or comments. General questions posted publicly are more likely to be addressed.

### Platform-Specific Guidelines

Each platform serves a different purpose:

**X (Twitter)**: Breaking news, quick updates, policy discussion, and engagement with current events

**Discord**: Community building, real-time discussion, event coordination, and member engagement

### Content Sharing

We encourage sharing our content. When sharing, please:

- Attribute the AWFixer Party
- Share in good faith for informative purposes
- Do not misrepresent our positions or use content in misleading ways

### Contact

For press inquiries, partnership proposals, or matters requiring direct attention, please use our official contact channels.
