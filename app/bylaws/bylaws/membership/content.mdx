## Membership Bylaws

These bylaws outline membership requirements and benefits.

### Article I: Membership Tiers

- Founding Members: Original organizers
- Active Members: Regular participants
- Associate Members: Supporting participants
- Honorary Members: Recognized supporters

### Article II: Dues and Fees

- Annual dues determined by board
- Waivers available for hardship cases
- First month free for new members

### Article III: Member Rights

- Voting rights per tier
- Access to member resources
- Invitation to all events
- Right to propose amendments
