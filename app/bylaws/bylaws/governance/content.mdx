## Governance Bylaws

These bylaws define the governance structure and leadership roles.

### Article I: Board of Directors

- Five members elected annually
- Terms are two years, staggered
- Board meets monthly

### Article II: Executive Officers

- Chair, Vice-Chair, Secretary, Treasurer
- Duties as defined in organizational charter
- Elections held each spring

### Article III: Committees

- Standing committees: Finance, Membership, Policy
- Ad-hoc committees as needed
- Committee chairs report to board
