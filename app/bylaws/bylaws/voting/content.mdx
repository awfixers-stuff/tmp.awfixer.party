## Voting Bylaws

These bylaws govern all voting procedures for organization decisions.

### Article I: Eligibility

- Members must be in good standing
- At least 30 days of membership required
- Voting rights are non-transferable

### Article II: Voting Methods

- Electronic voting for remote members
- In-person voting at designated locations
- Proxy voting allowed with prior authorization

### Article III: Quorum Requirements

- Minimum 10% of active members must participate
- If quorum not met, vote defers to following week
