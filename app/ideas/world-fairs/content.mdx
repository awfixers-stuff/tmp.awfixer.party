## The Vision

<HeroImage
  src="./images/aeral-view-colo.webp"
  alt="American World Fairs Exhibition Center"
/>

America's greatest moments weren't built for profit — they were built for pride. World Fairs brought together the boldest visions of the brightest minds, and the structures they left behind still define skylines today.

<QuoteBox>
  They built the Trylon and Perisphere when the world seemed infinite. We can
  dream that big again.
</QuoteBox>

## The Problem

We've stopped building monuments to the future. Our skylines fill with glass boxes, not visions. We need events that make Americans proud to be American again.

We've created a solution: reinvigorated World Fairs that celebrate American ingenuity and innovation while inspiring the next generation of builders.

<CalloutBox title="Built for Pride" variant="default">
  Every World Fair leaves behind lasting infrastructure. That's the point — we
  build to last, not to demolish.
</CalloutBox>

## How It Works

1. **Propose** - Submit a vision for American infrastructure
2. **Review** - Community feedback on feasibility
3. **Build** - If approved, construct for public benefit
4. **Legacy** - Leave something lasting behind
