## The Vision

<HeroImage src="./images/aeral-view-colo.webp" alt="American Gladiator Games Arena" />

America was built on competition. From frontier wrestling matches to backyard mud runs, Americans have always had an innate competitive spirit that, when properly channeled, builds community and discipline.

<QuoteBox>
The American spirit isn't soft — it's waiting to be challenged. Give it a worthy arena and watch what happens.
</QuoteBox>

## The Problem

Today's "extreme sports" are either:
- Too sanitized to be exciting
- Too dangerous to be safe
- Too expensive to be accessible

We've created a solution: organized gladiator games that honor America's competitive heritage while maintaining strict safety standards.

<CalloutBox title="Safety First" variant="default">
All events are professionally supervised with medical staff on-site. Safety gear is mandatory. The goal is competition, not casualties.
</CalloutBox>

1. **Register** - Sign up online or walk up
2. **Train** - 30-minute safety briefing included
3. **Compete** - Choose your event category
