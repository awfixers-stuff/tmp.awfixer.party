## Education Policy

**The Problem:**
American education policy has been trapped for decades in a binary debate between more funding (without accountability) and more testing (without addressing root causes). Neither side has moved the needle meaningfully.

**AWFixer Education Positions:**

### Restore True School Choice
- Current "school choice" often means choosing which underfunded public school your child attends, or whether you can afford private school.
- Support genuine choice: funding follows the student to any accredited option.

### vocational Pathways
- Not every student needs a four-year college degree. The systematic devaluation of vocational education has been a mistake.
- Support trade schools, apprenticeships, and career technical education with the same prestige as academic tracks.

### Teacher Independence
- Teachers are professionals, not implementing agents of federal programs.
- Support removing bureaucratic barriers that reduce classroom time to test preparation.

### Higher Education Reform
- Student loan debt has become a hidden tax on young Americans.
- Support common-sense reforms that prevent predatory lending practices.
- Encourage competency-based rather than time-based degree programs.

### The Core Principle
Education should prepare students for life, not just tests. The current system does the opposite for too many students.
